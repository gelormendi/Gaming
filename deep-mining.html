About Deep Mining

Deep Mining is a free browser-based idle strategy game where you take control of a lone miner on a quest to dig as far underground as possible. Armed with a pickaxe and an empty inventory, you navigate through layers of earth, rock, and stone — each hiding minerals, gems, and rare resources waiting to be discovered.

The game starts simple: move around, hit rocks, and collect whatever they drop. But as you descend, the terrain gets harder and the rewards get richer. Coal gives way to iron, iron to gold, and gold to exotic minerals that will fill your pockets with serious wealth.

Upgrade Your Way Down

Every mineral you collect can be sold for gold, which you reinvest into better pickaxes, larger inventory bags, and other upgrades. The progression loop is satisfying and addictive — the moment you can afford a new tier of equipment, deeper layers that felt impossible become reachable.

Manage Your Inventory

Your inventory fills up fast. Learning when to head back to the surface to sell — versus pushing for that one more vein of gems — is the core strategic tension of Deep Mining. Push too far and you'll miss out on valuable loot you can't carry. Play it too safe and you'll leave rare minerals behind.

No Download, Play Instantly

Deep Mining runs entirely in your browser. There's nothing to install, no account required, and no paywalls. Just open the page and start digging. It works on desktop and mobile browsers.
